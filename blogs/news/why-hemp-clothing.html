    Why we love our hemp clothing?

    For someone deep in the industry, like us, the answers are obvious. For our clients who better things to do than obsess about the materials that go into MouTHy products it may not be as apparent. So we'd like to give you a fairly brief rundown on why we're in love with hemp.

    Types of Hemp Clothing

    Pure 100% hemp

    Brand new pure hemp clothing can feel a bit picky or itchy if you have sensitive skin. However, with use it softens up considerably and becomes very comfortable.

    Hemp blends

    Hemp blends such as hemp/cotton, hemp/bamboo. hemp/silk or even hemp/polyester give you the best of both worlds right from the get-go.

    For example, a hemp/cotton blend, usually in a 55% hemp 45% cotton ratio gives you excellent wearability and comfort of cotton with the durability, antibacterial and uv blocking properties of hemp.

    Sustainability of Hemp

    Hemp is vastly superior to cotton in terms of energy inputs and water consumption.

    Hemp water requirement per KG of dry fiber: 300-500 liters 
    Cotton water requirement per KG of cotton: 8000-10,000 liters * 

    By using a 55/45 hemp/cotton blend we are eliminating the need for nearly 4500 liters of water necessary to make approximately 1 t-shirt!

    In addition hemp is a much more resilient cultivar than cotton and requires substantially less pest control and soil preparation work.






    Reference Links: Stockholm Environment Institute
